About US

The Bright Future Foundation, established under the Society Act 21, 1860, is a non-governmental organization dedicated to making a significant impact on the lives of vulnerable populations. Registered with certificate number 00245/2024-2025 and Pavli number M/BUL/0069166 on June 10, 2024, the foundation is officially listed under BUL/01627/2024-2025 with the Registrar of Societies.

Headquartered in Bulandshahr, Uttar Pradesh, the foundation is committed to addressing various critical issues through its multifaceted programs. One of its primary focuses is to support students and young girls by providing essential educational resources and opportunities. This initiative aims to empower them with the knowledge and skills necessary for their personal and professional development, thereby promoting educational advancement and gender equality.

In addition to educational support, the Bright Future Foundation works to alleviate food insecurity among impoverished children. By distributing meals and providing nutritional support, the foundation ensures that these children receive the necessary sustenance for healthy growth and development, helping to combat hunger and improve their overall well-being.

The foundation also addresses the needs of the elderly population, particularly those who are homeless. By offering shelter and care, the foundation provides a safe and supportive environment for senior individuals, ensuring they have access to basic necessities and a dignified living situation.

Through these dedicated efforts, the Bright Future Foundation strives to create a more equitable and compassionate society. Its programs reflect a deep commitment to social responsibility and a mission to improve the quality of life for marginalized and underserved communities.
